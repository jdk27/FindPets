Found a Lost Pet Form

This form is for pets who have been found and need to be returned to their rightful owner. If you have found a lost pet, and it does not match any of the pets reported missinghere, then please fill out the following information in as detail as possible. Please contact uniqueusers@gmail.com with further questions or concerns.

You will need a photo of the pet in order to post. Please post a photo of the pet only and use a clear (not blurry) picture. Additionally, please crop out any distractions such as people or other pets. The photo must be in image format (JPG, PNG).

**PLEASE NOTE: This form is specifically for pets lost/found in areas affected by natural disasters.** If you have found a pet outside of areas hit by natural disasters, please contact your local animal shelter.
